What's going on here?

This is an emotional simulation of a group of people waiting in line to get a video game. Every round, each of the people will have a chance to chose to pass the person in front of them. If they do, the person in front will decide if they want to try and stop them by protesting. After each round, the person at the front of the line will get the video game.

How are these decisions made?

Each person values their health, reputation and position the line at different levels. The more they like each specific resource, the more they'll try to protect it. You can see how their emotions change in the graphs next to their pictures.
